    About me

    I'm a charity administrator turned web developer. I've been teaching myself to build things on the web with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Node.js since 2019. I'm interested in all things tech and learning new technologies to solve real-life problems. My transition sparked while building WordPress websites and being curious about what was under the hood. I started engaging with the vibrant tech communities in London - Code First Girls and Codebar - where I coded alongside other devs to learn new languages.


    Currently, I'm finishing my last project for the Udacity Front End Nanodegree, working through the third freeCodeCamp certification for Front End Libraries to upskill in React and dipping my toes into the world of back-end. I have successfully completed two Code First Girls courses in Web Development and Python where I built projects with other inspiring women.


    I'm a keen learner who is looking to collaborate with like-minded, passionate people where diversity, personal development and feedback are valued.


    Feel free to follow my coding journey here where I am taking part in my second round of the #100DaysOfCode challenge and tweeting about what I'm learning daily.
